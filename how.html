GATHER

Devices today have made us expressive. We share how we feel using videos (onplatforms like Snapchat, Vine and Instagram), through Emoji in our text messages and by sharing selfies. These methods are the easiest and most natural way in humans can convey how they feel and tell their stories.

Samelogic gives Customers A Voice, literally, by giving customers a platform to tell their stories. This can’t be done effectively by using textboxes and radio buttons.

 

ANALYZE

The Snaps (videos) submitted by customers are then analyzed by Emotional Recognition Artificial Intelligence algorithms to measure if customers were happy, sad, upset, disgusted or surprised by your company.

 

REVIEW

Customer Experience teams can sort through these videos based on the emotions expressed by customers. Want to what made your customers happy? Click ‘Happy’ and you’ll be able to see why. This is perfect for meetings and is very actionable.
